# Vault Maintenance

## Lightweight checks

- Review unresolved wikilinks introduced by recent edits.
- Promote useful `inbox/` material into `working/`, `outputs/`, or `memory/`
  only after review.
- Distill durable insights from `daily/` and `outputs/` into `memory/` when
  appropriate.
- Archive or remove stale working notes when they no longer reflect reality.

## Bootstrap notes

Templates are vault-managed user state. They may be updated over time as the
user's workflow evolves.
